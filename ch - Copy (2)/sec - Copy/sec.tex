\section{\texorpdfstring{$\delta$}{Î´}-functors}
A functor $T=\{T_i\}_{i\in\bN}$ is a \emph{homological $\delta$-functor} if it acts like $H=\{H_i\}_{i\in\bN}$, the homology functor in the sense of 2.1 from \cite{Weibel_1995}.
